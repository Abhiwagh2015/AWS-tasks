how to copy AMI (Amazon machine image) to another account ?
============================================================

Ami can be copied in same regions .

Account 1:

1.go to AMI
2.select AMI > Actions > modify image permissions > | private 					   | >save
								    | paste Account 2 id , select check box  |

3. if you dont want to share image anymore then >select AMI > Actions > modify image permissions > deselect id 

Account 2 :

1. go to AMi
2. select private Ami
